Frequently asked questions

How does automatic knocking change its rhythm?

It knocks once per second for yourself, every 1.5 seconds for family, every 2 seconds for friends, and every 3 seconds for the world.

Why does automatic knocking stop when my screen is locked?

Lock-screen playback depends on continuous background music. Starting automatic knocking turns the music on. Turning it off, an audio interruption, or force-quitting the app stops automatic knocking.

How do I clear my count and local preferences?

Use “Reset merit” in the app settings to clear the count. Deleting the app also removes its locally stored preferences.

Does Muyu collect my data?

No. Muyu has no accounts, ads, tracking, or analytics. See the privacy policy.
