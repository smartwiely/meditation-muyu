Frequently asked questions

How does automatic knocking change its rhythm?

It knocks once per second for yourself, every 1.5 seconds for family, every 2 seconds for friends, and every 3 seconds for the world.

Why does automatic knocking stop while my screen is locked?

Automatic knocking uses background music for lock-screen audio playback. Turning music off, an audio interruption, or force-quitting the app will stop it.

How do I reset my count?

Use “Reset merit” in the app settings. Deleting the app also removes its locally stored preferences.

Does Muyu collect my data?

No. Muyu has no accounts, advertising, tracking, or analytics. Read our privacy policy.
