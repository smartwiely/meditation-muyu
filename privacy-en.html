Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Muyu respects your privacy. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

No personal data collection

Muyu does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data. The app has no account system, advertising, analytics, third-party tracking SDKs, or network requests.

Data stored only on your device

The app stores only your merit count, knock-sound setting, and background-music setting on your device to restore preferences. This information remains in the app’s private local storage and is not accessible to the developer. Deleting the app removes it.

Audio and lock-screen playback

When you start automatic knocking, Muyu plays background music to support audio playback while the device is locked. You can stop automatic knocking or turn off background music at any time.

Children’s privacy

Muyu does not collect personal data and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

Changes and contact

If this policy changes, the effective date on this page will be updated. For questions, contact ifan.wang.de@googlemail.com.
