Your privacy

Private by design.

Muyu is a quiet space for reflection. Your practice stays yours.

Effective July 13, 2026

01

No data collection

Muyu does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal data. There are no accounts, ads, analytics, third-party tracking SDKs, or network requests.

02

Stored on your device

Your merit count and app preferences are stored only in the app’s private local storage, so the app can remember them. They are not accessible to us. Deleting the app removes them.

03

Audio while locked

Automatic knocking can play background music to support audio playback while your device is locked. You can stop automatic knocking or switch off background music at any time.

04

Children and changes

Muyu does not knowingly collect children’s personal information. If this policy changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
